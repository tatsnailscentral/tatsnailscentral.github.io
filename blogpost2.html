Five "No" Before Going To Your Nail Tech


And did you know that the reason is not always the bad nail job? In this article you will learn what absolutely must not be done before going to the nail salon. These simple rules will help you not to ruin your nails and make the work of your nail tech easier.

Do not remove the old gel coating yourself. Remember: it is not gel polish that ruins the nails, but its improper removal. By doing this, you can damage the nail plate. Please refrain from doing your nails at home before going to the nail salon. Do not file the nails or trim your cuticles. In order to have long lasting gel manicure and avoid liftings, try not to apply cuticle oil on the day of the manicure, especially if you plan to have an E-File manicure. Please leave any nail issues for nail pros. Compliance with these simple rules will protect your hands from damage and, often, difficult-to-reverse consequences.
