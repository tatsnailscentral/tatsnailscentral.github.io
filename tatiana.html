Tatiana

Nail Professional & Business Owner

Tatiana discovered her passion for nail industry after many years of successful carrier in business finance and accounting while commuting to and from work in the busy Phoenix traffic and never having time to get her nails done! It started as curiosity, hobby, something fun to do and transitioned in the true passion for doing nails. Much so that she sought the official nail tech license after graduating from the Academy of Nail Technology in Phoenix, AZ in May 2020. About that time she also learned about the special Russian aka E-File aka Apparatus Technique so popular in European countries that she decided to travel to Russia to study the special method over there and she graduated from the Nail School Of Irina Sokolova in Volgograd, Russia in January 2021. Tatiana always was captivated with the idea of having her own business and this was where the dots connected and she dedicated herself to the full time nail tech job while also working on opening a nail salon in Central Phoenix.

About:

"I love and respect my profession. I believe that every woman deserves an ideal manicure and pedicure and every man deserves to be groomed. When I see happy eyes of my clients, I realize that I am in the right place doing the right thing. I always put my best effort forward whether I perform a regular manicure or some complex nail art-design. "
