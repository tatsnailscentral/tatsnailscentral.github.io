Manicure-2022: the main trends of the year

1 Bright stripes

Manicure and makeup in the spirit of the impressionists continues to excite the imagination of stylists and make-up artists (only the abstract artists are more popular today). We advise you not to complicate the task: arm yourself with multi-colored varnishes and a manicure brush and repeat the look from the Pyer Moss show. One and a half dozen bright careless strokes applied along (in height) the nail plate will not only look stylish and impressive, but also visually lengthen the fingers.



A more recent solution is to keep alignment not with the classics of the past, but with their contemporaries. Decorate your nails with this graffiti painting!
